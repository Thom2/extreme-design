In the context of the events organized by the universities for EXPO, you are invited to the poster session of the work made from international researchers working at Politecnico di Milano in the different schools of engineering, architectural and design fields.

Researchers (from Canada, China, Estonia, Germany, Greece, India, Iran,...) will present with short talks their projects related to innovation and the topics of EXPO (such as: nutrition, sustainability, research and development).
